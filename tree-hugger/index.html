Tree Hugger
Submission for the ArchTriumph TreeHouse Competition

The “Tree Hugger” project was designed for the Arch-Triumph Treehouse Competition. The idea behind the this treehouse is to structure it in such a way that it causes minimal to no damage to the tree, but keeps that treehouse/cabin feel that people look for when vacationing in the Redwoods. I worked on the entire production, from design the treehouse and rendering in Keyshot, to the final presentation.
